About JAIN COLLEGE

Jain College is one of the top colleges in Bangalore, providing education at the Pre-University, Under Graduate and Post Graduate level.

Established in 1990 by Dr. Chenraj Roychand. It is the flagship Institute of Jain Group of Institution. Spread over 4 campuses, the college is home to more than 8000 students and helped in educating millions of young minds hailing from more than 35 countries.
