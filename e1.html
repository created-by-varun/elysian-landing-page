The Blagger

(Debate)

It’s better to change an opinion than to persist in a wrong one. It takes courage and confidence to stand for what’s right, and you do it through your debating skills. When you hit rock bottom, you still have a way of making it through the abyss to get a point across. After-all what’s more human than the fight for the pursuit of right ideas and rigorous arguments?
